Home page
About page
Product page
order page

Home Page: The home page should have the following features- navigation to other pages, link to your socials, hero banner for the product you sell, hero text describing the product/services, main, footer

About page: tell us about your products and services, stating your vision and mission statement

Product page: the product page should have at least 6 cards.
The product card should have the product title, description, price and buy button.

Order page: the order page should have a form to place order
